## I. Subject Matter and Purpose

1. Due to the expansion of the hub data center and the upgrade of the departure control system of **北航**, Party A purchases the following equipment from Party B for use in the computer rooms at **北方航空**’s Guangzhou headquarters and Shanghai Hongqiao base.
2. The specifications of this batch of equipment are aligned with those of the concurrent tender of **东南航** (中国东南航空股份有限公司) and are provided only for technical benchmarking purposes; they do not constitute any rights or obligations of **东南航** towards Party A.

## II. Equipment List (Summary)

| No. | Category      | Specification Summary                             | Quantity |
| --- | ------------- | ------------------------------------------------- | -------- |
| 1   | PC Server     | 2U rackmount, dual CPU, ≥512GB memory             | 40 units |
| 2   | Core Switch   | 100G uplink, redundant power supplies             | 8 units  |
| 3   | Access Switch | 25G downlink, POE+                                | 24 units |
| 4   | Firewall      | Next-generation firewall, dual-device hot standby | 2 sets   |

## III. Delivery and Acceptance

1. Party B shall deliver the goods to the warehouse designated by **北航** (Guangzhou) no later than 2026 年 6 月 30 日.
2. Party A has the right to entrust a third-party inspection agency to participate in acceptance; if the specifications do not conform to the contract, **北方航空** may refuse to accept the goods and require Party B to replace them within 15 days.

## IV. Price and Payment

The total contract price including tax is RMB **壹仟贰佰捌拾万元整** (¥12,800,000.00). Party A shall make payment in two installments: 40% after contract signing, and 60% after the goods have passed acceptance.

## V. Confidentiality and Publicity

Without the prior written consent of **华云智联**, **北航** shall not disclose Party B’s quotation details in press releases; nor may Party B publicize externally using wording such as “**北航**’s exclusive supplier”.

## VI. Miscellaneous

This Contract is executed in four originals, of which **北方航空** holds two and **华云智联** holds two, all of which have the same legal effect.
